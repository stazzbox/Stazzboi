The Right Hand Alliance

Isaac "Beachy Bird" Ochoa is the chief executive partner to President Kelvin Stazzland. Their structural alignment forms the central pillar of Stazzerian governance:

  • Strategic Counterpart: Translates Presidential vision into functional national governance.
  • Principal Advisor: Functions as the President's closest advisor and operational sounding board.
  • Executive Continuity: Serves as the ultimate stabilizing safeguard beneath the Presidency.

Authority & Oversight

Beachy Bird wields substantial executive oversight across governmental branches. Under the Stazzerian hierarchy, the Vice President holds direct supervisory authority over the Monarch, making the office responsible for maintaining operational systems and requiring the Monarch to report directly to him.
