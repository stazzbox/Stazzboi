Stazzboi

Stazzboi is the stage name of Kelvin Stazzland, a Stazzerian-American musician, content creator, and digital entrepreneur based in California. He is known for building a growing online presence through music releases, branded media channels, gaming promotion, and the Stazzerian ecosystem.

His music catalog includes releases such as Locked In, Like That, Up Next, and Swerve, available across streaming platforms including Spotify. He also operates multiple content outlets including official YouTube channels, clips pages, and social media networks.

Stazzland is also the founder and President of Stazzeria and the creator of Stazzology, a gaming promotion platform featuring tournaments, events, and team brands such as Frost, Valor, Kingdom, and Integrity.
