STAZZBOI

Content Creator • Music Artist • Digital Entrepreneur

Kelvin “Stazzboi” Stazzland is known for his social media content covering topics such as private security, overviews of Stazzeria, and commentary-based hot takes. Stazzland began creating content on YouTube in 2013 under previous names including KID14XD, BLACKFOXARK, and most notably KELVIN TV before later adopting the name STAZZBOI (Stazz Boy). His early content primarily focused on gaming titles such as Call of Duty: Black Ops II, Grand Theft Auto V, and Minecraft, later expanding into content surrounding Call of Duty and Rainbow Six. Over time, his content shifted away from gaming and toward IRL-style media including vlogs, reactions, and opinion-based videos on various topics.

THE KHAMELION

Kelvin Stazzland is associated with several titles, with “The Khamelion” being among the most recognized due to its meaning and construction. The nickname was reportedly given to Stazzland by members of his inner circle and has been described as a reflection of his ability to adapt within environments — particularly social settings that may initially appear uncomfortable — while maintaining a composed and effortless presence. In a published article, Stazzland stated that he views his “Khamelion state” as the ability to blend into situations, events, societal settings, or environments where he may not naturally fit in or fully agree with the surrounding norms.

BIOGRAPHY

Kelvin Stazzland, born September 15, 1998, and identified within the Stazzerian Tribe, currently resides in California. He is the founder and operator of Stazzology, a gaming promotion platform created as an homage to the origins of his content career, which began in 2013. Stazzology continues to maintain a presence within the Call of Duty community, with a primary focus on Call of Duty: Mobile content. On April 11, 2026, Stazzology hosted “Bites and Battles,” a competitive gaming event held at MegaBites Arcade in Hemet, California.
