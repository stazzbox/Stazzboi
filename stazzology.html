Stazzology

Stazzology is a gaming promotion and media platform created by Kelvin “Stazzboi” Stazzland.

The platform focuses on tournaments, digital competition, events, community engagement, and branded gaming content across multiple platforms.

Stazzology serves as the gaming division of the broader Stazzerian ecosystem, connecting entertainment, competition, and creator culture.
