Stazzology

Stazzology is a gaming promotion, tournament, and digital media platform founded by Kelvin “Stazzboi” Stazzland, also known as The Stazzerian President and The Khamelion.

The platform focuses on tournaments, digital competition, community engagement, creator culture, gaming media, and live entertainment initiatives connected to modern Stazzerian culture.

Stazzology is recognized for events such as Bites and Battles @ Megabites Arcade in Hemet, California, helping establish a recurring identity within local gaming and creator communities.

As a media and gaming initiative associated with Stazzeria, Stazzology combines competition, branding, creator promotion, and entertainment-focused productions across multiple platforms.
