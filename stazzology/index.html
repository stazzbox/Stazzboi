Bites and Battles

Bites and Battles is one of the most recognizable live STAZZOLOGY competitive events associated with CODM tournament culture and Stazzerian gaming entertainment.

Hosted in Hemet, California at MegaBites Arcade, the event combines esports competition, faction rivalries, live community interaction, and organized Stazzerian entertainment presentation.

The event is strongly associated with The Aimbot Kelvin “Stazzboi” Stazzland and the expansion of STAZZOLOGY competitive identity.
